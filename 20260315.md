# Grammar

## Pei
`pei` means to accompany someone or stay with them.

## Structure
<subject> pei <person>

## Examples
wo zai zher pei ni: I am here with you
pei ni de nver: accompany your daughter

## Ba + Verb + Qilai
Used for handling something and bringing it into a completed or gathered-up state.

## Structure
<subject> ba <object> <verb> qilai

## Examples
ni keyi ba yifu die qilai ma?: Can you fold the clothes up?
ba sheng cai fang zai bingxiang li: put the leftovers in the refrigerator
