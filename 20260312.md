# Basic Sentences
zai shuo yi ci: say it again
wo mei qu guo nanjing: I have never been to nanjing

# Guo/Le (Have Experienced / Have Completed)
Guo/le  = have done before / completed; already did

## Examples
Wo chi le: I ate
Wo qu le: I went
Wo qu guo Zhongguo: I have been to China
Wo qu guo Nanjing: I have been to Nanjing
Wo chi guo ma po dofu: I had ma po tofu before
Wo mei qu guo Yidali: I have never been to Italy



# Putting/Placing Things

## Structure
<subject> ba <thing> fang zai <place>

## Examples
Wo ba yifu fang zai xi yi ji li: I put clothes in the washing machine
Wo ba shouji fang zai zhuozi shang: I put my phone on the table
Bie ba ni de wanju luan fang: Don't leave your toys everywhere
daochu dou shi ni de wanju: your toys are everywhere
hen luan: messy
daochu hen luan: it's messy everywhere

# Do/Don't do two things at once

## Structure
(bie) yibian (first thing) yibian (second thing) 

## examples
bie yibian chi fan yibian kan dianshi: don't eat while watching tv
bie yibian kaiche yibian da dianhua: don't drive while making a call
